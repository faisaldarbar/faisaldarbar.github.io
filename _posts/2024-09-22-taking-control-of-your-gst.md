# Taking Control of Your GST Filings: A Lesson from My Experience

Managing GST can be tricky, especially if you rely on someone else to file your returns. I used to leave everything to my tax consultant, which led to some serious issues with my business. In this video, I walk you through what went wrong and how I turned things around.

## Why This Matters:
- **Your Responsibility**: Even if a consultant handles the paperwork, you’re accountable for accurate returns.
- **Lost Input Tax Credit**: Failing to properly record your purchases can prevent you from claiming tax credits.
- **Using the Right Tools**: Software like Zoho Books can help streamline the GST process and let you review everything before it’s filed.

In the video, I dive deeper into the challenges I faced and share tips on how you can avoid them. Watch it to ensure you don’t make the same mistakes.
